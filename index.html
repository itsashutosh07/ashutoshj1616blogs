FCB fan for life

FC Barcelona is a Spanish professional football club based in Barcelona, Catalonia. Founded on 29 November, 1899 and is one of the most widely supported teams in the world, and the club has one of the largest social media following in the world among sports teams. The club has become a symbol of Catalan culture and Catalanism, hence has the motto "Més que un club" ("More than a club"). Unlike many other football clubs, the supporters own and operate Barcelona, also the club is the world's richest football club in terms of revenue, with an annual turnover of €840.8 million.

The Club has it's own football ground popularly known as "Camp-Nou" an has a capacity of 99,354 people making it the largest in Spain and 3rd largest in the World. The club also has it's own Youth Traning Academy known as "La Masia". In 2010, La Masia became the first youth academy to have trained all three finalists for the Ballon d'Or in a single year - Andrés Iniesta, Lionel Messi and Xavi. Barca's playing style is known as 'tiki-taka', which loosely translates to “touch-touch” and refers to the rapid, precise passing style that allows the team to maintain possession for large portions of the match, was instilled onto the team's foundation by the Legendary Barca Manager - Pep Guardiola. Fan's are called as ‘Culers’ and are very passionate towards the team and it's players.

Since childhood, I played football more entusiastically than any other sports. I was personally a big Leo Messi Fan, even long before I started following the Barça team. So when I had to choose a team for suppoting, barça became my top option. Now, I follow the team with my whole-heart <3 .
